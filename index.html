Who we are?

Kasing Lung is the Belgium-based artist who created Labubu. Originally born in Hong Kong, Lung moved to the Netherlands at a young age. It was in 2015 that he published “The Monsters” story series. Lung has since become a prominent artist and has had exhibitions all over the world.

What we offer


Labubu, a character from the “The Monsters” series by Kasing Lung, offers a variety of collectible
items through POP MART. Here are four popular offerings:




VINYL FIGURES

These are detailed, collectible figures featuring Labubu in various themes and poses, such as the “Superstar Dance Moves” figure.



PLUSH DOLLS

Soft and cuddly plush versions of Labubu, like the “Best of Luck” vinyl plush doll.




BAGS

Stylish and functional bags, including plush tote bags and backpacks featuring Labubu designs.




THEMES ACCESSORIES

Items like earphone cases and canvas bags with Labubu’s unique design.
